    Education

    I have 3 degrees in different fields from different universities in different countries with different cultures.

    • Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering, Alfaisal University, Saudi Arabia.

    • Master of Science in Industrial and Systems Engineering, Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, South Korea.

    • Doctor of Philosophy in Compter Science, University of New South Wales, Australia.

    My PhD research was on developing new algorithms for accelerating topology optimization algorithms. All of my PhD research was implemented in an open source software package TopOpt.jl.

    Work Experience

    During my PhD, I worked as a part-time Julia programmer in the Turing.jl project. Currently, I am a senior product engineer and consultant at PumasAI Inc.. I am also a casual research affiliate at the University of Sydney Business School.
